This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are and how we use them on GameHub. You should read this policy to understand what cookies are, how we use them, and how to manage them.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off.
  • Performance Cookies: These allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve site performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: These enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.
  • Targeting Cookies: These may be set through our site by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that remain in your browser until you leave the site.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain in your browser for a set period of time or until you delete them.
  • First-party Cookies: Set by our website.
  • Third-party Cookies: Set by a third party (like Google Analytics).

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.
